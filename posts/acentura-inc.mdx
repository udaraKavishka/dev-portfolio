I recently had the opportunity to attend the **IEEE StudPro 8.0 Industry Connect session**, hosted at **CINEC Campus Malabe**, featuring a collaborative session with **Acentura Inc.** — a leader in delivering digital transformation solutions. This event was a remarkable opportunity for students and young professionals to gain firsthand insights into industry practices, career building, and the evolving landscape of technology.

### About Acentura Inc.

Acentura Inc. is recognized for its innovative contributions across various sectors such as manufacturing, telecom, insurance, and finance. Their expertise spans a wide array of cutting-edge technologies and solutions, including:

- ✅ **ERP Integrations**
- ✅ **Advanced Planning & Scheduling**
- ✅ **Cloud & Digital Transformation**
- ✅ **Business Intelligence & Analytics**
- ✅ **Custom Software Engineering**

Their approach to technology not only accelerates business growth but also fosters resilience in rapidly changing digital environments.

### Career Wisdom from Industry Leaders

The session featured two powerful and thought-provoking discussions led by senior representatives from Acentura.

#### Chandana Ranasinghe — CEO, Acentura Inc.

Mr. Ranasinghe delivered a compelling talk centered around **career flexibility and unconventional growth strategies**. Some of the key takeaways included:

- **Seek the path less taken** — Instead of following the crowd, identify unique gaps in the market and position yourself strategically.
- **A real-world success story** — He shared the journey of an individual who achieved success by trusting their instincts rather than following mainstream trends.
- **Adaptability over conformity** — In a fast-evolving industry, those who remain agile and strategically minded will always have the edge.

His session was a refreshing reminder that success doesn’t always come from doing what everyone else is doing — often, it’s about carving your own path.

#### Shamilka Samaranayake De Alwis — Talent Acquisition & HR Strategy Lead

Shamilka’s session was a masterclass in **career development** and navigating the professional world. Her insights were especially valuable for students preparing to step into the workforce. Highlights included:

- **Resume Crafting Tips** — How to tailor your CV to specific roles, highlight relevant skills, and maintain a polished presentation.
- **Interview Best Practices** — Emphasizing the importance of doing your homework about the company, communicating clearly, and asking thoughtful questions.
- **Learning from Rejection** — Understanding that rejection isn’t the end; it’s a stepping stone. Her advice on leveraging feedback for continuous improvement was particularly impactful.
- **The Growth Mindset** — She stressed the value of resilience and adaptability, especially when things don’t go as planned.

### Final Thoughts

The **IEEE CINEC Student Branch** deserves a huge shoutout for organizing such a meaningful event. It was more than just a career session — it was a space for real conversations, honest advice, and transformative learning.

Acentura Inc.’s willingness to share insights from both executive and HR perspectives made this Industry Connect session incredibly well-rounded. For all **aspiring tech professionals**, the message was loud and clear:

> *“The future belongs to those who can adapt, innovate, and carve their own path.”*

Here’s to embracing change, thinking differently, and building careers that stand out!
